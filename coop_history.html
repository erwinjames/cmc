What is Cooperative?

A cooperative is a duly registered association of PERSONS, with a common bond of interest who have voluntarily joined together to achieve a lawful common social or economic end, and making equitable contributions to the capital required and accepting a fair share of the risks and benefits of the undertaking in accordance with universally accepted cooperative principles.

Cordova Multipurpose Cooperative History

How CMC Started?

October 30-31 & November 1, 1971

Rev. Fr. Rudy Abao, MSC initiated Self-discovery Seminar to 25 Parishioners

November 15 to December 11, 1971- 1st PMES to 25 participants was conducted

December 12, 1971

1st Organizational Meeting elected officers of the cooperative or the ad-hoc officers, initial capital was P250.00

March 14, 1972

Registered as Pre-coop at the Cooperative Administration Office with the name CORDOVA COOPERATIVE CREDIT UNION

March 14, 1972

Registered as full-fledged Coop with the name CORDOVA CREDIT COOPERATIVE INC(CCCI)

July 5, 1978

Consumer Store was opened, due to members demand the capital was from memberâ€™s dividend & patronage refund

July 9, 1981

Consumer store was registered as a separate coop and was named as CORDOVA CONSUMERS COOPERATIVE INC. (CCONCI)

June 19,1993

Consolidation of CCCI & CCONCI with the new name CORDOVA MULTIPURPOSE COOPERATIVE

December 13, 1993- Opening of Lapu-lapu Branch

March 28, 2007 - Opening of Basak Branch

October 30, 2008 - Opening of Olango Branch

September 8, 2016 - Opening of Mandaue Satellite Office
