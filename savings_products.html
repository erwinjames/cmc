PRODUCTS AND SERVICES / COOP SAVINGS

Fixed Deposit

Share Capital
• It is considered as fix investment in the coop.
• Yearly Dividend
• Initial deposit of PHP 300.00
• Non-withdrawable
• One of the bases for loan evaluation.
• A PHP 50.00 charge in case of close account

Savings Products

REGULAR SAVINGS
• Open to all Regular Member
• Open account for at least PHP 200.00
• Bears a competitive interest of 4% per annum
• NO monthly charges
• Withdrawable from Monday – Friday during official banking hours (8:00-3:00)
• Interest earned is not subject to withholding tax

Time Deposit

TIME DEPOSIT
• Open to all regular & associate member
• Minimum amount for this account is PHP 5,000.00
• Bears a competitive interest rate base on the amount placed upon the bracket amount and term.
• Renewable upon maturity and interest can be withdrawn monthly.
• Pre-termination is allowed but the rate to be used in computing the interest
• Shall be based on the rates above.
• Interest earned is not subject to withholding
